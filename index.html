Who I am

A short public portrait.

I am a multidisciplinary technology entrepreneur and solo technical founder operating across France, Turkey and the UK. My journey started with a fascination for how technology can transform lives, and over two decades it has grown into a portfolio of companies and products that aim to do exactly that.

In France, I run Netz Informatique, an IT-services company and certified training provider in Haguenau. In Turkey, I lead Reflektif Bilişim A.Ş., an AI-powered psychometric career-discovery platform. In the UK, I operate CortexAI. Alongside these, I build independent products such as BilanCompetence.AI and mikail.ai.

“Technology should amplify human potential, not replace it.”
